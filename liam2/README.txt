1. Download LIAM-2 from http://liam2.plan.be/
2. From the downloaded folder, put "liam2/" inside the "hello_world_paper/liam2/" folder.
   We excluded it due to size constraints
3. Open the application "editor/Notepad++Portable"
4. Within the app
    a. Open "scripts/model_import.yml" and hit F5
    b. Open "scripts/run_model.yml" and hit F6
5. To model the intervention, change the variable INTERVENTION on line 60 of "run_model.yml" to True
